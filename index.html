The Solution

AI-Guided X-Ray Vision for Construction

01

Scan the Wall

Workers use their smartphone or tablet to scan any wall surface. Our BLE-based detection system identifies hidden MPE components in real-time.

02

AI Real-Time Guidance

AI analyzes what the worker sees and provides instant voice guidance: "Stop—water pipe 3 inches behind this spot" or "Safe to drill here—clear zone ahead."

03

See Through Walls

Augmented reality overlay shows exact locations of pipes, wires, and ducts. Like X-ray vision powered by building data and AI.

The AI Advantage

Instead of requiring years of experience to understand building systems, workers get instant AI coaching:

"Water main detected 4 inches ahead—do not drill"
"Electrical conduit runs vertically here—drill horizontally instead"
"Clear zone—safe to proceed with anchor installation"
"This valve controls water to floors 3-5—tag before work"

Multimodal AI can see, reason, and guide. Workers become effective immediately, accessing expert-level knowledge on demand.
